2017 2 Accountancy And Business Year 4 (Risk Management And Insurance)
BR2204 INTRODUCTION TO FINANCIAL RISK MANAGEMENT 4.0 AU
Prerequisite: BR2201
Mutually exclusive with: BF2209, BU9226
This course provides students with an overview of the standard instruments used in financial risk and credit risk management, and emphases on their applications in hedging risks and creating various risk profiles consistent with the investor's expectations of asset price movements. It covers recent capital regulation for financial institutions.
 
BR2205 INSURANCE PRACTICE 4.0 AU
Mutually exclusive with: BA3206, BU9225
This course provides students the basic principles and legal aspects of insurance. The topics include the principles and practices of underwriting, insurance broking and claim management.
 
BR3203 PRINCIPLES OF ENTERPRISE RISK MANAGEMENT 4.0 AU
Prerequisite: BR2201
Mutually exclusive with: BU9227
This course provides an introduction to the main areas of enterprise risk management (ERM).The industry accepted categorisation of risks, i.e. market risks, credit risks and operational risks, and components of each category will be discussed in detail. Risk management processes and strategies will also be covered. Numerous case studies from different business areas and projects on real life issues will illustrate the increasing importance of ERM in today?s business world.
 
BR3204 PROPERTY & LIABILITY INSURANCE 4.0 AU
Prerequisite: BR2202
This course provides students practical knowledge and skills for underwriting and claim management in property and liability insurance.
