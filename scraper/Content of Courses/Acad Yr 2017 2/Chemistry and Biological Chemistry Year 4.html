CM4014 NANOSCIENCE & NANOTECHNOLOGY 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: By permission
Prerequisite: CM3011
This course provides a platform to understand small systems, in particular, materials at the nanometer length (10-9 m). In the last few decades, we have witnessed the progress and rise of Nano Age. This course provides the foundation and understanding of this multidisplinary field, which is the convergence of chemistry, materials, physics, biology and etc. In this course, we will study how nanoscience and nanotechnology help solving global challenges faced by mankind.
 
CM4015 INTRODUCTION TO ATMOSPHERIC CHEMISTRY 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: By permission
Prerequisite: CM2011 & CM2062 & CM3062
Mutually exclusive with: ES4903
The atmosphere contains numerous chemical species, those react each other. The course will cover chemistry and transport of those species. Impacts of atmospheric chemistry on global climate and local environments will also be discussed.
