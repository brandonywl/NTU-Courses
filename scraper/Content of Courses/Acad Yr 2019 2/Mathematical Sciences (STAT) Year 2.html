HW0128 SCIENTIFIC COMMUNICATION I 2.0 AU

This course aims to develop fundamental mathematical concepts such as definite integrals and their applications to find areas and volumes, Fundamental Theorem of Calculus, integration techniques, tests for convergence and divergence of sequences and series, interval and radius of convergence of power series, differentiation and integration of power series, and Taylor series.
