IM2004 SIGNALS & SYSTEMS 4.0 AU
Prerequisite: IM2092 OR
MH1810 & MH1811 OR
MH2810
Mutually exclusive with: EE2010
Through this course, students should be able to understand the representation of continuous-time and discrete-time signals; their frequency characteristics and Fourier spectrum; representation and characteristics of linear time-invariant systems in both time and frequency domains; and the principles of sampling a continuous-time signal to yield a discrete-time one. Course content: Signals and Systems. Linear Time-Invariant Systems. Fourier Representation of Signals and LTI Systems. Sampling. Modulation.
