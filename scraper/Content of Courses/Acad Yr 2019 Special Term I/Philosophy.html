HY8001 PHILOSOPHY FOR EVERYONE 3.0 PHIL(SOH)
Mutually exclusive with: HY9001
This course covers a broad range of perennial philosophical issues in metaphysics, epistemology, aesthetics, logic, social philosophy, political philosophy, and ethics. It may also covers some specialized subjects such as philosophy of special sciences, philosophy of religion, and philosophy of technology.
