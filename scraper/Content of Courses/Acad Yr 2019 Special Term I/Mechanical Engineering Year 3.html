MA3003 HEAT TRANSFER 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: MA2007
Mutually exclusive with: AE3003, MP3003
Not available to Programme: ME(2015-onwards), ME(DES)(2012-onwards)(Non Direct Entry), ME(DES)(2013-onwards)(Direct Entry), ME(RMS)(2012-onwards)(Non Direct Entry), ME(RMS)(2013-onwards)(Direct Entry)
A comprehensive knowledge of heat transfer is essential to the training of a mechanical engineer in our knowledge-based economy. As a first course on the subject, it covers the essential topics of heat transfer viz. one-dimensional, steady and unsteady heat conduction, extended surface heat transfer, convection fundamentals and applications, principles and methods of heat exchanger rating and design and radiation heat transfer analysis.
 
MA3004 MATHEMATICAL METHODS IN ENGINEERING 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: MH1810 & MH1811 OR
CY1203 OR
MH2812 OR
CY1602 OR
MH2810
This course covers introductory material on PDEs, FEM and CFD and is arranged in three parts. The first part covers classification of PDEs and typical analytical methods for solving them. The second part covers basic finite element concepts and typical applications in solid mechanics. The third part covers basic concepts in CFD with a focus on finite volume methods.
 
MA3006 FLUID MECHANICS 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: MA2003
Mutually exclusive with: MP2005, MP2305
Momentum equation and its applications. Dimensional analysis and similitude. Internal flows and piping systems. Principles and applications of fluid machines.
