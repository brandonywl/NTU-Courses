HE2011 LABOUR ECONOMICS & LABOUR RELATIONS 3.0 ECON(SSS)
Prerequisite: HE1001 OR
HE9091 OR
AB0901
It covers the determination of labor market demand and supply, wage determination, wage differentials, human capital investment, migration and discrimination, as well as unemployment and labor policies.
 
HE4013 EXPERIMENTAL ECONOMICS 4.0 ECON(SSS)
Prerequisite: HE2001
This course introduces the methodology of experimental economics and its application to specific topics. The main themes are: 1. Designing economic experiments 2. Conducting economic experiments 3. Analysing experimental data 4. Applications to time preferences, risk preferences, and social preferences
