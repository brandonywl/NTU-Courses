CH1131 BIOMOLECULAR ENGINEERING 4.0 AU
Mutually exclusive with: CH1031
The course will cover the basic and functional aspects of cellular systems. Analysis of components and their functionality in cellular systems is included in this course. Students will also be exposed to aspects of biotechnology, genetic and metabolic engineering which play an increasingly important role in the new era of chemical engineering.

Module I : General Introduction Module 2: The Global Seeding of Oil Palm Module 3: Recent Expansion of Oil Palm in South East Asia: How and Why did this happen? Module 4: How is Oil Palm Production Affecting Environment, Climate and Human Society? Module 5: Making Oil Palm More Sustainable Module 6: General Conclusions Module SI: National Geographic (Nat.
