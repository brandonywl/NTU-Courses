COURSE CODE TITLE AU PROGRAMME/(DEPT MAINTAIN*)
HF9101 INTRODUCTION TO CHINESE CREATIVE WRITING 3.0 CHIN(SOH)
1. Explore and analyze various genres of works, such as fiction, prose, poetry, playwriting, intermediate creative writing etc. and develop their working knowledge. 2. Select at least one literary work in each of the categories in fiction, prose, poetry, playwriting and intermediate creative writing for students to learn how to appreciate and discuss. 3. Students are to choose two genres of works within these categories to write. 4. Each student is to submit two pieces of works, where interpretation, discussion and critics of the works should be included in the presentation. Students are to make revision to the writing by incorporating comments/inputs from their course mates and lecturer before submitting the works for final assessment.
 
HF9601 CREATIVE WRITING: CHINESE CROSSMEDIA WRITING 3.0 CHIN(SOH)
1. Explore and analyze various styles of crossmedia writing and develop a practical exposure to a variety of technical devices that are specific to the crossmedia writing. 2. Select famous crossmedia writing for students to learn how to appreciate and discuss. 3. Each group of students must create two crossmedia texts. They should display and explain them in an oral report. Each crossmedia text must be uploaded to new media and published so that audiences may critique it. The students who created the texts should be able to interact with audiences using new media. 4. Students are to make revision to the writing by incorporating comments/inputs from their course mates and lecturer before submitting the works for final assessment.
