CV0003 INTRODUCTION TO DATA SCIENCE & ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: CV1014
Mutually exclusive with: CE1015, CE4073, CH0494, CZ1015, CZ1016, CZ4073, MA0218, MS0003
Not available to all Programme with: (Admyr 2011-2017)-Non Direct Entry, (Admyr 2011-2018)-Direct Entry
In today's era of Information, `Data' is the new driving force, provided we know how to extract relevant `Intelligence'. This course will start with the core principles of Data Science, and will equip you with the basic tool and techniques of data handling, exploratory data analysis, data visualization, data-based inference, and data-focussed communication. The course will also introduce you to the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence - state space representation, uninformed search, and reinforcement learning. Python will be the language of choice to introduce hands-on computational techniques.

HY0001 ETHICS & MORAL REASONING 1.0 AU
Grade Type: Pass/Fail
Not available to Programme: DSAI
HY0001 will introduce students to three major ethical theories' utilitarianism, Kant's deontology, and virtue ethics. Then, four weeks will be devoted to teaching the ethical principles underlying academic integrity, research ethics, and intellectual property. Finally, students will discuss issues related to the ethics of environmental sustainability and conservation. All the while, students will be challenged apply the ethical theories learned to concrete moral problems, including world poverty, corporate accountability and whistleblowing, and workplace discrimination.
